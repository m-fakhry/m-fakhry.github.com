About

Mohamed Ghalwash is a Research Scientist at IBM T.J. Watson Research Center, NY USA. He is also an Associate Professor of Computer Science at Ain Shams University.

Ghalwash is interested in developing machine learning and AI models for discovery of interpretable spatio-temporal patterns from complex events and exploiting their utilities in practical high impact real-world applications, focusing on analytics for healthcare, health behavior, decision support, and to support the generation of real world evidence from medical data, wearable devices and instrumented environments.

My long-term goal is to put my footprints in the field of pattern recognition through contribution of original ideas and translating those ideas to end applications and products.
